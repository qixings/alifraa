About Alifraa

Alifraa is an Indian export business that specializes in providing high-quality natural rubber and other essential agricultural products to international markets. Our offerings include premium-grade natural rubber as well as staple Indian goods such as onions, potatoes, and other locally sourced products. At Alifraa, we are committed to delivering sustainable, top-quality exports while ensuring our products meet the diverse needs of our global clients. Our focus on integrity and reliability allows us to build long-term relationships with international partners, supporting them with a wide range of products sourced from India’s rich agricultural and industrial sectors. Starting from natural rubber, our vision is to expand our portfolio, making Alifraa a trusted name in the global export industry.
